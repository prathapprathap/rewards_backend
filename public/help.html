Frequently asked questions

How do I earn reward points?

Complete daily check-ins, finish tasks and offers from the offerwall, scratch surprise cards, enter special codes, and refer friends. Every completed action adds points to your wallet.

How do I withdraw my balance?

Open My Wallet, tap Withdraw, enter your UPI ID and the amount. Once verified, the cash is sent directly to your UPI account.

How long do withdrawals take?

Most UPI withdrawals are processed quickly after verification. During high demand it may take a little longer — you can track the status under My Redeems.

How does Refer & Earn work?

Share your referral code from the Refer screen. When a friend installs the app and signs in using your code, you both earn rewards.

I didn't get my reward for a task. What should I do?

Some offers take time to confirm with the provider. If a reward hasn't appeared after a while, email us with your registered email and the task name and we'll check it for you.

Where can I read the privacy policy?

You can read our full Privacy Policy any time.
